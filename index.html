Welcome

Sam Sharpe Teachers' College Quality Assurance Office

Welcome to Sharpe EduQuality Connect, the digital home for quality assurance, communication, evidence gathering, and continuous improvement at Sam Sharpe Teachers' College. This platform supports students, academic staff, administrative staff, ancillary staff, department heads, and management by making quality processes easier to understand, access, and act on.

Why Quality Assurance Matters

Quality assurance helps the college maintain high standards in teaching, learning, student support, institutional accountability, compliance, and service delivery. It gives every stakeholder a voice, strengthens decision-making, and helps the college provide evidence of improvement for ISO 21001, J-TEC, JBTE, TCJ, and Ministry requirements.
